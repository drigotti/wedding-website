Ceremony & Reception


The ceremony and reception will be held at

Liberty Warehouse
260 Conover St
Brooklyn, NY 11231
(347) 987-3121

The Liberty Warehouse is located on Pier 41 in the historic shipping yards of Red Hook, Brooklyn. The building was constructed in the pre-Civil War 1850′s and has recently been renovated into an event space. Located directly on the water’s edge, there are expansive views of New York Harbor. The Liberty Warehouse is the only location in all of New York where the Statue of Liberty is face front as she looks on to France.
